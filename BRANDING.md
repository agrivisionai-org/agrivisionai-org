# BRANDING

Brand guidelines for AGRIVISION AI. Apply these across repositories, READMEs, product surfaces, and external communications.

## Brand Essence

**AI-first technology for the world's farmers.**

## About

AGRIVISION AI is an AI-first technology company building intelligent products for global agriculture. Our flagship platform, **YieldAI Global**, delivers AI-powered crop intelligence to agricultural extension workers across India, Africa, Latin America, and Southeast Asia -- 70+ languages, 40+ countries.

Founded by **Vijesh Reddy Golamari** (ex-Google, ex-Meta, ex-Citi, ex-Flipkart), with five years building production AI.

## Brand Voice

We sound like a founder writing in public -- clear, technical, and grounded in the field.

**We are:**
- Authentic -- we ship, we document, we admit what's hard.
- Founder-led -- first-person, specific, accountable.
- Mission-driven -- agriculture matters; we don't need to shout about it.

**We are not:**
- Corporate -- no "synergies," no "leveraging paradigms."
- Preachy -- we don't lecture about saving farmers.
- Hype-y -- no "revolutionary," no "disrupting," no rocket emojis.

Default to short sentences. Name the technology (Gen AI, RAG, voice agents). Show the work.

## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Primary Green | `#0F6B3E` | Logo, primary buttons, headings on light backgrounds |
| Secondary Green | `#5DBB63` | Accents, success states, illustrations, data viz |
| Accent Blue | `#0A84FF` | Links, interactive elements, AI/intelligence cues |
| Cream | `#F4F6F1` | Page backgrounds, cards, soft surfaces |
| Ink | `#0B1410` | Body text (use on Cream, never pure black) |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Reverse text, contrast surfaces |

Primary Green is load-bearing -- every surface should carry it somewhere. Reserve Accent Blue for moments that signal intelligence (model output, AI agents, smart insights).

## Typography

- **Display / Headings:** Poppins -- weights 600 and 700. Used for H1-H3, hero copy, product names.
- **UI / Body:** Inter -- weights 400, 500, 600. Used for paragraphs, UI labels, code annotations, captions.
- **Code:** system monospace stack (`ui-monospace, SFMono-Regular, Menlo, monospace`).

Never mix display weights below 600. Never set Inter above 600 for body copy.

## Logo

**Do:**
- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "A" on all sides.
- Use the Primary Green mark on Cream or White backgrounds.
- Use the white reverse mark on Primary Green or Ink backgrounds.
- Keep minimum size at 24px height (digital) / 12mm (print).

**Don't:**
- Recolor outside the approved palette.
- Stretch, skew, rotate, or add drop shadows / outlines.
- Place on busy photography without a solid backing plate.
- Recreate or re-letter the wordmark in another typeface.

## Naming Conventions

- The company is always written **AGRIVISION AI** -- all caps, space between words, no hyphen, no camel case.
  - Not: Agrivision AI, AgriVision.AI, AGRIVISION.AI, Agrivision-AI.
- The flagship product is **YieldAI Global** -- title case, "AI" capitalized, single space.
- In code, repos, and handles, use lowercase `agrivisionai` (e.g., `agrivisionai-org`, `@agrivisionai`).
- First mention in any document uses the full name **AGRIVISION AI**; subsequent mentions may use **AGRIVISION**.
- Product feature names use sentence case: "AI crop advisory," "weather and pest alerts," "mandi price signals," "automated field visit reports."
