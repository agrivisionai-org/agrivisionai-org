We're an AI-first technology company building intelligent products for global agriculture.

Our flagship platform **YieldAI Global** is an AI-powered crop intelligence system built for the agricultural extension workers and farming households who feed the world. Designed for India Africa Latin America and Southeast Asia , across 40+ countries and 70+ languages.

Extension workers are the backbone of global food security each one advises 1,200+ farming households. Yet most work without real-time data, AI tools, or automated reporting. YieldAI changes that.

## What we're building

- **AI crop advisory** Gen AI + RAG over agronomic knowledge
- **Live weather + pest and disease alerts** proactive, localized
- **Real-time mandi prices vs MSP** sell/hold signals
- **Automated field visit report generation** from raw voice notes
- **4 specialized AI agents** crop advisor, market intelligence, weather, field visit
- **Multilingual voice-first AI** Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Swahili, Arabic, Portuguese, Bengali, and 60+ more

## Why this exists

Agriculture is the largest industry in the world. It feeds 8 billion people. And it's the most under-served by modern software.

We believe every farming household from Iowa to Andhra Pradesh to Lagos to So Paulo deserves access to AI-powered agricultural intelligence. Regardless of language. Regardless of income.
